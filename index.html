Robby Bobby the Robot - GTMK Gam Jam 2023 - July 2023

Out of the dozen of game jam I entered, this one is the only one I succesfully completed. And it's for a good reason, I wouldn't have made it without my buddy Arkonny / Foxrox ! What an amazing experience this was. The them e was "Role Reversed", so we decided that the player would control the enemies, which turned out in a pretty janky, but fun game ! Also, as the only coder, I decided to use the very niche "Lobster Programming Language", as I wanted to learn it for a long time and thought this was the perfect occasion. It was.

Play me on itch.io !

Controls :
Q/D : move around
Z/S : switch controlle enemy
Protip : Be sure to take the power ups that randomly spawn on the map, they're very helpful ! And you want to PROTECT the blue thing in the middle, by SUICIDING the enemy you control onto the sword !

Xelov - a limited voxel editor - August 2022

In 2021 I joined Gabe Rundlett's voxel centered community, and as a result, a year later, I couldn't resist making my own voxel thinghy. So here it is, a little voxel something (although technically not cubic voxel, it stills count for me as it is rendering a 3D grid volume). I was strongly inspired by Sebastian Lague a few years prior.

Play me in the browser !

Controls :
Left Click : orbit
Scroll : zoom
Right Click : move
Mouse 4 / Key A : dig
Mouse 5 / Key B : build
